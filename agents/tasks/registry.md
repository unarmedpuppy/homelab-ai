## Task Status Legend
- `pending` - Available to claim
- `claimed` - Claimed by agent, not started
- `in_progress` - Actively being worked on
- `blocked` - Waiting on dependencies
- `review` - Needs review
- `completed` - Finished
- `cancelled` - Cancelled
